IRCEvent.Type

dialect defs IRCEvent

Types of IRCEvents.

Taken from https://tools.ietf.org/html/rfc1459 with many additions.

Some are outright fabrications of ours, like CHAN and QUERY. These are not prefixed with RPL_ and ERR_ (but not all such are made up, like AWAY and CHGHOST that are properly AWAY and CHGHOST).
